About Us

Yestoal. is an individual's business that was found in 2021 and is on the process to be run within an informal settlement of Kibera. The owner is a Deaf person who has the interests of other people in the community at heart and is wishing to serve them better with the best quality and affordable services requires by customers. Customer-satisfaction is all he is desiring to provide to all the customers of Yestoal.
